Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first session.

How much do you charge? expand_more

Pricing depends on the size, detail, and placement of the design. My minimum starts at €150. Custom projects are quoted individually after the consultation.

Do you do cover-ups? expand_more

I do limited cover-ups if they align with my fine line and botanical style. Send a photo of your existing tattoo for evaluation.

How should I prepare for my appointment? expand_more

Make sure you are well-rested, hydrated, and have eaten a meal. Avoid alcohol for 24 hours prior. Wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the tattoo area.
